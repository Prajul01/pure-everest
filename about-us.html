About Pure Everest

Bringing authentic Nepali flavors to the world with uncompromising quality, traditional methods, and modern standards since 2020.

Our Journey Began in the Heart of Nepal

Pure Everest was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to share the authentic taste of Nepal with the world. Born from a deep love for traditional Nepali cuisine and a commitment to preserving our cultural heritage, we started as a small family business in Kathmandu.

Our founders, having experienced the disconnect between authentic Nepali flavors and what was available internationally, decided to bridge this gap. We began by carefully selecting traditional recipes passed down through generations and sourcing the finest ingredients directly from local farmers and producers across Nepal.

Today, Pure Everest has grown into a trusted exporter of premium Nepali food products, serving customers in Japan, the UK, and expanding to new markets worldwide. Yet, we remain true to our roots – every product tells a story of Nepal's rich culinary tradition.

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide us in bringing authentic Nepali cuisine to tables around the world.

Our Mission

To export the finest authentic Nepali food products worldwide while preserving traditional flavors, supporting local farmers, and maintaining the highest quality standards. We strive to be the bridge between Nepal's rich culinary heritage and global food enthusiasts, ensuring that every product carries the authentic taste of the Himalayas to international kitchens.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading exporter of premium Nepali food products, making authentic Himalayan flavors accessible globally while empowering local communities. We envision a future where Pure Everest products are found in every international kitchen, representing the finest of Nepal's culinary excellence and sustainable farming practices.

Our Values

Authenticity in every recipe, quality in every grain, sustainability in every process, and respect for tradition in every product. We believe in fair trade, supporting local farmers, environmental responsibility, and building lasting relationships with our customers through trust and transparency.
